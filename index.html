Technology should stay quiet
until it actually matters.

People no longer use technology intentionally. They react to it.

Messages. Notifications. Alerts. Infinite feeds.

Attention became fragmented.

Why We Started

We noticed something strange.

People disable notifications just to feel calm again.

Technology designed to help people
started competing for attention instead.

Instead of showing everything,
what if technology showed
only what matters?
